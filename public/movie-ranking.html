My top 3 movies of all time

Casino Royale (James Bond 007)

Best dialogue (arguments) between JB and Bond girl in the train about the other's habit, character, presumable family background and heritage. Another argument when the Bond girl is taking the hotel elevator, rejecting JB to join her in saying: "There is not enough room inside here for both me and your ego."

Jesus of Montreal

source: wikipedia:
The film tells the story of a group of actors in Montreal who perform a Passion play in a Quebec church (the film uses the grounds of Saint Joseph's Oratory on Mount Royal), combining religious belief with unconventional theories on a historical Jesus. As the church turns against the main actor and author of the play, his life increasingly mirrors the story of Jesus, and the film adapts numerous stories from the New Testament.

What happens in Vegas

source: wikipedia:
It stars Cameron Diaz and Ashton Kutcher as a couple who get married and win a casino jackpot prize during a drunken night in Las Vegas, but their simple plan to get a quick divorce and divide the money is complicated by the divorce court judge's ruling.
